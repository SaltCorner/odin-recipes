Macaroni and Cheese

Description

A gooey and delicious meal, or a fantastic side to compliment any main dish, macaroni and cheese remains a staple on many tables to this day. Between a rich cheesiness and a toothful noodle, the essence of this dish lies directly in its simplicity.

It is this simplicity that allows for many creative takes on this classic dish, whether it be a bit of spice or the addition of different proteins to round out the flavor profile, many will jump to get a bowl or plate of even this simple take on the classic

Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray
  • 2 cups Uncooked Elbow Macaroni
  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • 2 cans (or 12 ounces) Evaporated Milk
  • 1 tablespoon Ground Black Pepper
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 4 cups Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 pinch Paprika (Optional)
